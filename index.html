INTERSPEECH 2021

Target Speaker Text-to-Speech Synthesis from a Face Image Reference using Global Style Token Embedding


Björn Plüster✝ Leyuan Qu Cornelius Weber Stefan Wermter

Knowledge Technology Group (WTM)
Department of Informatics, University of Hamburg

Abstract

With multi-speaker TTS models from voice reference showing promising results and a crossmodal association of face and voice becoming more and more evident, we pose the question to what extent it is possible to predict a target speaker's voice from a face image reference. This paper proposes a model architecture combining Tacotron 2 multi-speaker TTS with a FaceNet face encoder using an intermediate representation based on Global Style Tokens to predict a speakers voice and speaking characteristics from a face image reference. Experimental results show reasonable matching ability while retaining good voice naturalness. We examine these results, compare with other methods, and discuss multiple possible avenues of improvement.
